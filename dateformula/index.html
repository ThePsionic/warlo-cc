Dynamics NAV / Business Central DateFormula Tester

Sometimes it may be difficult figuring out how a DateFormula will behave, especially if working with complex formulas or using dates far into the future or the past.
This tool will help you by automatically parsing DateFormulas you insert into it and giving you the result for your reference date.
